FLOWBORN

The Peaceful Fist

"They don't know where they learned any of this. But the moment a fight starts — their body moves."

FULL RUNTIME · 0:00 — 4:10
0:00 – 0:12

Hook & Identity

"They walked out of Jixia Academy with no memory of how they learned to fight — and no idea what they're capable of. Doesn't matter. The moment a teammate needs covering, their body moves on its own. They don't need to understand the flow inside them. They just need to feel it — and trust it. Whether you're anchoring the solo lane or roaming as a support, the approach is the same — feel the Flow, and protect what matters. In this guide, I'll show you how to read the rhythm, chain the controls, and turn Flowborn into the Peaceful Fist."
0:12 – 0:55

Skill Showcase

"Flowborn's kit is built around one resource and one idea: build up, then unleash.

Passive — Flow of the World

At the base, Flowborn selects a weapon to enhance their stats: Vitality for HP and both defenses, Piercing Heart for HP and physical attack, Haste for HP and movement speed, Focus for HP and cooldown reduction. Beyond that, Flow is the fuel for everything. Spend one stack on an enhanced basic attack — it deals bonus physical damage and heals you, both scaling with your max HP. Even in full tank gear, those enhanced basics hit hard and keep you alive.

Skill 1 — Convergence

Flowborn channels the Flow — halving other skills' cooldowns, gaining up to 9 stacks of Flow, and recovering HP. Every nearby teammate and every two minions add an extra stack and more healing. It also knocks back enemies in the inner radius. This is your recharge tool — pop it near your wave or your team and you come out fully loaded.

Skill 2 — Power From Within

Charges at a locked-on target, knocking back units in the path, slashes for physical damage, and gains a shield for 3 seconds. Chain it onto the same target and the crowd control escalates: second cast launches them briefly, third cast launches them longer. Spend 4 Flow stacks to reset the cooldown and fire again without waiting. Skill 2 can also lock onto targets through walls — useful for chasing or escaping.

Ultimate — All In

Flowborn leaps to a target location, knocks back enemies, and deals physical damage with 50% bonus at the center. Spend 5 Flow stacks to land a second time. Queue any skill the moment you cast it — it fires automatically on landing, and Convergence skips its pre-animation entirely, so you get instant crowd control the frame you touch down."

0:55 – 1:45

Core Resource & Combo

"Here's how it all ties together. Convergence is how you fill Flow — ideally near your wave or teammates to hit the 9-stack cap. Hold those stacks until you're ready to spend them in one clean burst: Power From Within three times, All In twice, or a chain of enhanced basics for sustained damage and healing.

Weapon choice shapes each game. Piercing Heart when you have lane dominance — your enhanced basics become a genuine threat. Haste when you need to roam — it covers the movement speed gap your kit can't solve alone. Vitality when you're taking heavy burst — the double-resistance keeps you standing. Default to Piercing Heart; switch to Haste when you need to roam or chase.

Enhancement effects are your late-game flex. Winning? Flip to Enhanced Damage — close the game faster. Losing? Flip to Enhanced Defense — stay alive longer and buy your carries more room.

Skill priority: Ultimate first, then Skill 2. Leave Convergence last — its cooldown reduction is fixed and it's always available at base level.

Summoner spells: Flash is the standard — it opens tower-dive combos and bails you out of bad spots. Enfeeble for lane bullying; Execute when you want to finish carries yourself."

1:45 – 3:20

Advanced Mechanics — Offense, Defense & Combos

Offensive Rhythm

The basic lane combo is Convergence into two Power From Withins into an enhanced basic — one, two, two, charged hit. Fast, reliable, and lethal on any hero without a dash.

In teamfights, open with Convergence, queue All In immediately so it lands with instant crowd control, lock onto their carry with Power From Within, weave in enhanced basics, then spend remaining Flow on a second All In if they try to escape. The sequence: One, Ult, Two, enhanced basics, second Ult.

For tower-dives, open with All In plus Flash to land behind the target, Convergence to recharge, then double Power From Within to knock them clear of the tower.

Both Power From Within and All In pass through most terrain — aim at a wall, watch for the blue landing-light on the other side, and go through. Use this to chase through river walls or escape through brush walls. If the blue light doesn't appear, the wall is impassable.

Defensive Rhythm

Flowborn's weakness is movement speed — they can be kited. The fix is patience: never open Power From Within at max range on an approaching enemy. Wait until they're close — the knockback travels much farther at point-blank. Keep Convergence ready to halve your cooldowns in a lull. And remember your wall-passage is an escape route just as much as an engage tool — Power From Within through the nearest wall hands you a shield and distance in the same cast.

In the support role: your priority is always the carry. Knock the diver off first, chain Power From Within to keep them away, then worry about yourself."

3:20 – 3:50

Best Teammates & Tough Matchups

"Flowborn covers the movement speed weakness with the right partners. Sun Bin's recovery buffs let Flowborn soak more damage and stay in fights longer. Da Qiao's repositioning keeps Flowborn at full health so they can front-line without backing off. Any teammate who brings speed boosts makes the Haste weapon choice even more decisive.

Best targets: heroes without a dash. Once Flowborn locks on and chains their skills, they have nowhere to go — Hou Yi is the textbook example.

Tough matchups: heroes with multiple crowd controls, like Lady Zhen, can stack debuffs on Flowborn before they can respond. Lu Bu with his enchantment deals true-damage burst that cuts straight through tank stats. Against either, play slow — farm your Flow near the wave, stay close to your carry, and let the fight come to you."

3:50 – 4:10

Closing & Retention Hook

"They don't know where they learned any of this. But the moment a fight starts — their body moves. Build the Flow. Land in the middle. Chain the controls until no one can reach your carry. That's what it means to be the Peaceful Fist. And that's Flowborn. I'll see you in the next episode."
